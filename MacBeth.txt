<h1 style="text-align:center">Chapter 2.</h1>
<h2 style="text-align:center">MacBeth</h2>
<br>
<br>
<p>Double, double toil and trouble;</p>
<p>Fire burn and caldron bubble.</p>
<p>Fillet of a fenny snake,</p>
<p>In the caldron boil and bake;</p>
<p>Eye of newt and toe of frog,</p>
<p>Wool of bat and tongue of dog,</p>
<p>Adder's fork and blind-worm's sting,</p>
<p>Lizard's leg and howlet's wing,</p>
<p>For a charm of powerful trouble,</p>
<p>Like a hell-broth boil and bubble.</p>
<br>
<p>Double, double toil and trouble;</p>
<p>Fire burn and caldron bubble.</p>
<p>Cool it with a baboon's blood,</p>
<p>Then the charm is firm and good.</p>
